# Expert (NPC Class)

The expert's primary focus is with techniques such as Fuinjutsu, Genjutsu and Ninjutsu. This makes him a fair substitute for an enemy ninja, or a non-ninja who dabbles in the art.

## Class Information
**Hit Die:** 1d6
**Action Points:** 1 per level
**Skill Points:** (6 + Int modifier) x 4 at 1st level; 6 + Int modifier at each level thereafter.
**Class Skills:** Chakra Control (Wis), Concentration (Con), Decipher Script (Int), Disable Device (Int), Drive (Dex), Fuinjutsu (Int), Genjutsu (Cha), Listen (Wis), Knowledge (behavioral sciences, earth and life science, history, ninja lore, physical sciences, popular culture, tactics, theology and phylosophy) (Int), Ninjutsu (Int), Perform (Cha), Read Language (None), Search (Int), Sign Language (None), Speak Language (None), Spot (Wis).

## Starting Feats
The Expert begins play with the Simple Weapon Proficiency feat and proficiency in light armors.
