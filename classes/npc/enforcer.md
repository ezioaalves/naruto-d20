# Enforcer (NPC Class)

The enforcer is a strong fighter despite his lack of refined skills some better trained warriors amass. This class is best used for melee combatants such as soldiers, thugs and brutes, or even enemy ninja of a lesser calibre.

## Class Information
**Hit Die:** 1d8
**Action Points:** 1 per level
**Skill Points:** (2 + Int modifier) x 4 at 1st level; 2 + Int modifier at each level thereafter.
**Class Skills:** Balance (Dex), Craft (structural), Climb (Str), Concentration (Con), Drive (Dex), Genjutsu (Cha), Jump (Str), Knowledge (ninja lore, streetwise, tactics) (Int), Listen (Wis), Read Language (None), Ride (Dex), Speak Language (None), Spot (Wis), Survival (Wis), Swim (Str), Taijutsu (Str) and Tumble (Dex).

## Starting Feats
The Enforcer begins play with the Simple Weapon Proficiency feat and proficiency in light and medium armors.

## Class Features

### Bonus Feats
At 5th, 10th, 15th and 20th level, the Enforcer gains a bonus feat which it may select from the Strong or Tough hero bonus feat lists.
