# Grand Scholar

The grand scholar is a master of the more technical aspect of techniques. His unique  abilities enable him to uncover secrets none other have access to, and make him  particularly suited to imparting their craft on others.
## Requirements:
To qualify to become a grand scholar, a character must fulfill all the following criteria. 
* **Skills:** Knowledge (arcana) 3 ranks, Linguistics 3 ranks, and at least 1 ranks in any four of the following skills: Chakra Control, Fuinjutsu, Genjutsu, Knowledge (any), Ninjutsu or Taijutsu.
* **Feats:** Grand Master.
* **Techniques:** The grand scholar must know at least 10 techniques from three different technique types, four of which must be rank 3 or higher.

## Class Information
The following information pertains to the Grand Scholar advanced class.

* **Hit Die**: The Grand Scholar gains 1d6 hit points per level. The Constitution modifier applies.
* **Action Points**: The Grand Scholar gains a number of action points equal to 6 + one-half his character level, rounded down, everytime he attains a new level in this class.
* **Class Skills**:The Grand Scholar's class skills are as follows. Chakra Control (Wis), Craft (Int), Diplomacy (Cha), Fuinjutsu (Int), Genjutsu (Cha),  Knowledge (any) (Int), Linguistics (Int), Ninjutsu (Int), Perception (Wis), Perform (Cha), Profession (Wis), Sense Motive (Wis), Survival (wis), Taijutsu (Str).
* **Skill Points at Each Level:** 9 + Int modifier.

### Loremaster
At 2nd level, the grand scholar gains a bonus to all Knowledge and Research checks equal to one-half his grand scholar level.

### Research Technique
At 4th level, the grand scholar can research a particular technique extensively to gain intimate knowledge of it. This ability provides two benefits.
Each time the grand scholar gains a level in this class, he can select one technique he  knows as his perfected technique. The grand scholar treats his effective character level when performing his perfected technique as though being 2 higher. In addition, the character gains a +2 bonus to master this technique, which stacks with the Grand Master feat.
Furthermore, the grand scholar can apply his Linguistics skill when learning, developing and creating technique. This process requires additional time equivalent to an attempt made to learn the technique, modified by a Linguistics check against the technique's Learn difficulty (therefore, exceeding it by a sufficient amount can lower the time this process takes). A successful check grants the grand scholar a bonus to Learn checks with the technique equal to one-half his grand scholar level. In addition, if the grand scholar was creating or developing a technique, his effective character level to determine the additional number of successes is increased by 2.
### Grandmaster
At 6th level, the grand scholar can pass on his knowledge to others with far greater efficiency than even the most capable, non-scholar teachers. When determining the maximum bonuses he can spread amongst multiple students, add double his grand scholarlevel to the pool, though no student can receive a bonus greater than +5. When teaching a single individual, the bonus that student receives, regardless of its level in relation to the grand scholar, increases by +2. In addition, the grand scholar can use this ability to aid another character trying to create or develop a technique. To do so requires a Research check equal to the technique's difficulty, and grants the character a bonus to Learn checks equal to one-half the grand scholar's level. This requires the same time commitment as teaching a technique.

### Chakra Reader
At 8th level, the grand scholar can concentrate and read chakra by touch. This enables him to determine the chakra signature of a touched creature as an attack action as though using sense chakra, or identifying the properties of a touched item as though using the Chakra Reading effect of the Chakra Shori no Jutsu technique by concentrating for 5 minutes.

### Grand Discovery
At 10th level, the grand scholar can enter a state of absolute insight and clarity once per day. This state lasts for 1 minute, but enables him call information to mind that would normally take hours of research to find. As a free action once per round, the grand scholar can study a creature or object and glean information regarding it as though he had rolled 15 on any Knowledge and Linguistics checks concerning it, adding his modifier for that particular skill normally.
In addition, the grand scholar identifies any curious quirks of energy surrounding the object or creature. This enables him to gain information as though looking at the subject with the see through chakra ability, and to identify any property it might have as though with the chakra reading effect of the Chakra Shori no Jutsu technique, including the subject's elemental affinities and nature transformation if it has any, without having to make a check.
