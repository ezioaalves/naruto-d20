# Pain Engine

The pain engine is little more than a killing machine. For her, entering a battle frenzy is transcending into an euphoric state of pure, unadulterated joy and she feels nothing but pleasure as she goes about wreaking havoc, raising chaos and general mayhem.

## Requirements

To qualify to become a pain engine, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Base Attack Bonus**: +10.
*   **Feats**: Cleave, [Universal: Risky Strike], Rampage.
*   **Special**: The pain engine must have the rage class feature.
*   **Allegiance**: The pain engine cannot have the law allegiance, or any allegiance that might prevent her from acting out her whims.

## Class Information

The following information pertains to the Pain Engine prestige class.

*   **Hit Die**: The Pain Engine gains 1d12 hit points per level. The Constitution modifier applies.
*   **Action Points**: The Pain Engine gains a number of action points equal to 7 + one-half his character level, rounded down, everytime she attains a new level in this class.
*   **Class Skills**: The Pain Engine's class skills are as follows: Climb (Str), Intimidate (Cha), Jump (Str), Ride (Dex), Swim (Str).
*   **Skill Points at Each Level**: 3 + Int modifier.

## Class Features

The following features pertain to the Pain Engine prestige class.

### Frenzy
The pain engine's frenzy is fueled by pain. Whenever she suffers any damage, the pain engine must enter frenzy at the beginning of her next turn by spending a swift action. The only way to avoid this is by making a Will save (DC 15+damage taken). Furthermore, during the first round of the frenzy, the pain engine must target the creature that triggered the frenzy with at least one attack, or move as close to that creature as she can if it is not already in range. At the beginning of her turn each round, the pain engine is compelled to attack a creature she perceives as a foe to the best of her ability. If she runs out of targets before the frenzy ends, her rampage continues and she attacks the nearest creature regardless of friendship, innocence or health. The only way to prevent this is by ending the frenzy.

During the frenzy state, the pain engine gains a +4 bonus to her Strength score, and can make an additional attack at her highest attack bonus anytime she makes a full-attack action (this effect is not cumulative with other effects that grant additional attacks). The pain engine suffers a –4 penalty to Defense, however, and cannot use Intelligence-, Wisdom- or Charisma-based skills, the [Universal: Defensive Stance] feat, or use any ability or perform any task that requires attention or concentration. In addition, the pain engine suffers 2 points of nonlethal damage at the start of each round during her frenzy.

The frenzy lasts for 3 rounds, plus the pain engine's Constitution modifier. The pain engine can attempt to end her frenzy as a free action at the beginning of her turn once per round by making a Will save (DC 20). This ability can be used a second time per day at 4th level.

### Frenzied Rampage
At 2nd level, the pain engine becomes exceedingly capable at tearing things and people apart in a single blow. Whenever she charges during her frenzy, she can apply the benefits of the [Universal: Risky Strike] feat as though she had lowered her attack bonus by an amount equal to her pain engine class levels. This doesn't allow the pain engine to gain more power attack damage than she would normally be able to lower her attack bonus by (for example, a 4th level pain engine with a base attack bonus of +16 could lower her attack bonus by –6 during a charge and gain the benefits of having lowered her attack bonus by –10).

### Inspire Frenzy
At 3rd level, the pain engine's euphoria is contagious and can sometimes draw in neaby allies who yield to it. Once per day when the pain engine enters frenzy, she can draw in all willing allies within 10 feet, who then gain all the effects of her frenzy, including the negative effects, as though they had the ability themselves. The frenzy of the affected allies last a number of rounds equal to 3 plus the pain engine's Constitution modifier, even if they move further away than 10 feet from her.

### Euphoric Frenzy
At 5th level, the pain engine's frenzy gives her such immense pleasure that she becomes completely indifferent to effects that would cause her to falter, and to pain. During her frenzy, the pain engine is immune to fear and pain effects.

### Greater Frenzy
At 5th level, the pain engine gains a +6 bonus to Strength instead of +4 during frenzy.
