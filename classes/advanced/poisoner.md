# Poisoner

While poison is a staple in certain kinds of warfare, only a select individuals have the capacity to bring out its full potential in both its creation and its application in combat. The poisoner is a true master at his craft and can be a ruthless enemy to have, because of his ability to strike virtually anyone down at any time, anywhere, without actually having to be nearby. To say the least, despite the heavy stigma surrounding his profession, the poisoner is not someone to cross lightly.

## Requirements

To qualify to become a poisoner, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Skills**: Craft (chemical) 8 ranks, Craft (pharmaceutical) 5 ranks, Treat Injury 5 ranks.
*   **Feats**: Craft Poisons.

## Class Information

The following information pertains to the Poisoner prestige class.

*   **Hit Die**: The Poisoner gains 1d8 hit points per level. The Constitution modifier applies.
*   **Action Points**: The Poisoner gains a number of action points equal to 7 + one-half his character level, rounded down, everytime he attains a new level in this class.
*   **Class Skills**: The Poisoner's class skills are as follows: Craft (chemical, pharmaceutical) (Int), Concentration (Con), Disable Device (Int), Drive (Dex), Hide (Dex), Knowledge (current events, earth and life sciences, physical sciences, popular culture, streetwise, technology) (Int), Listen (Wis), Move Silently (Dex), Profession (Wis), Read/Write Language (none), Research (Int), Search (Int), Sleight of Hand (Dex), Speak Language (none), Spot (Wis), Treat Injury (Wis).
*   **Skill Points at Each Level**: 7 + Int modifier.

## Class Features

The following features pertain to the Poisoner prestige class.

### Swift Poisoner
The poisoner gains a bonus to Craft (chemical) check equal to the poisoner's class level when creating poisons. In addition, the poisoner can craft poisons in 75% the normal amount of time.

### Poison Expert
The poisoner never risks poisoning himself when applying a dose of poison to a weapon.

### Prolific Poisoner
At 2nd level, the poisoner becomes more efficient when crafting poisons. After making a successful Craft (chemical) check, the poisoner crafts 5 doses of poison instead of 4.

### Enhanced Poisons
At 3rd level, the poisoner creates his poisons much more effectively than normal. He can take a –5 penalty to Craft (chemical) checks when creating a poison to create an enhanced poison instead. This enhanced poison has increased effects, but only when applied and delivered by him personally; in the hands of anyone else, the poison behaves normally. In doing so, the poisoner only creates 3 doses of the poison.

The enhanced poison deals its primary damage immediately, then secondary damage after 1 minute, but it also deals damage after 5 rounds, which can be either primary damage or secondary damage, at the poisoner's choice. This damage still provokes a saving throw from the target normally. For example, enhanced heartoot powder might be created to deal 1d4 points of Constitution damage immediately, then 2d4 points of Constitution damage after 5 rounds and again after 1 minute.

### Poison Immunity
At 4th level, the poisoner becomes immune to all poisons.

### Master Poisoner
At 5th level, the poisoner can spend an action point to inject, ingest or otherwise inflict himself with a dose of poison as an attack action to enhance some of his abilities, or as an instant action when affected by a poison effect or ability. The effects of this ability depends on the effect of the poison, and the effects last for 1 minute if the poison was applied by the poisoner, or 5 rounds if it came from another source. If the poison has multiple different effects, the poisoner must chose which one he gains benefit from.

*   **Strength Damage or Drain**: The poisoner gains a +4 enhancement bonus to his Strength score.
*   **Dexterity Damage or Drain**: The poisoner gains a +4 enhancement bonus to his Dexterity score.
*   **Constitution Damage or Drain**: The poisoner gains a +4 enhancement bonus to his Constitution score.
*   **Intelligence Damage or Drain**: The poisoner gains a +4 enhancement bonus to his Intelligence score.
*   **Wisdom Damage or Drain**: The poisoner gains a +4 enhancement bonus to his Wisdom score.
*   **Charisma Damage or Drain**: The poisoner gains a +4 enhancement bonus to his Charisma score.
*   **Fatigue or Exhaustion**: The poisoner becomes immune to fatigue and exhaustion.
*   **Paralysis**: The poisoner becomes immune to paralysis effects.
*   **Sickened or Nausea**: The poisoner becomes immune to the sickened and nauseated conditions.
*   **Unconsciousness**: The poisoner becomes immune to sleep effects.
*   **Lethal or Nonlethal Damage**: The poisoner gains 2d10 temporary hit points.
*   **Chakra Damage or Drain**: The poisoner gains 2d6 temporary chakra.
