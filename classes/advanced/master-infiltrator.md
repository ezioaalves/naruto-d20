# Master Infiltrator
The master infiltrator as a well-deserved reputation of being able to infiltrate any  structure, no matter how secure. Despite his lack of shinobi skills, the master infiltrator can mask his presence completely, scale virtually any surface in the blink of an eye, and do so without attracting any attention. The fastest path to this class is through the Fast hero basic class.

## Requirements:
To qualify to become an master infiltrator, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
* **Skills:** Acrobatics 3 ranks, Climb 3 ranks, Stealth 5 ranks
* **Feats:** Dodge

## Class Information
The following information pertains to the Master Infiltrator advanced class.
* **Hit Die**: The Master Infiltrator gains 1d8 hit points per level. The Constitution modifier applies.
* **Action Points**: The Master Infiltrator gains a number of action points equal to 6 + one-half his character level, rounded down, everytime he attains a new level in this class.
* **Class Skills**:The Master Infiltrator's class skills are as follows. Acrobatics (Dex), Climb (Str), Disable Device (Int), Escape Artist (Dex), Perception (Wis), Profession (Wis), Ride (Dex), Sleight of Hand (Dex), Stealth (Dex), Swim (Dex).
* **Skill Points at Each Level**: 5 + Int modifier.

Class Features
The following features pertain to the Master Infiltrator advanced class.
Acrobatics
At 1st level and on, the master infiltrator gains a +1 bonus to Balance, Climb, Jump and 
Tumble checks whenever he wears light armor or no armor. This bonus increases by +2 
every 4 master infiltrator levels thereafter.
In addition, the master infiltrator can spend 2 chakra as a swift action to count as though 
having a running start on his next Jump check or take an action for accelerated climbing 
for 1 round. This use of chakra is considered to be concealed (see Chakra Control skill for
details.)
Uncanny Dodge
At 2nd level, the master infiltrator retains his Dexterity bonus to Defense when caught 
flat-footed or struck by an unseen opponent. The benefits of this ability do not extend to 
feinting or other conditions that may deny the master infiltrator his Dexterity bonus to 
Defense.
Trackless Steps
At 2nd level and on, the difficulty to follow the master infiltrator's tracks increases 
exponentially. The DC to follow the master infiltrator's tracks with the Survival skill 
increases by +1. This effect stacks with the increased difficulty if the master infiltrator 
chooses to hide his trail. This bonus increases by +2 every 3 master infiltrator levels 
thereafter.
Sneak Attack
This ability functions as the Ninja Scout sneak attack ability. If the master infiltrator can 
catch an opponent when he is unable to defend himself effectively from his attack, he can 

strike a vital spot for extra damage. Creatures immune to critical hits and sneak attacks, 
or who have concealment, do not suffer extra damage.
This extra damage is 1d6 at 3rd level, and it increases by 1d6 every 3 levels thereafter. If 
the master infiltrator gains sneak attack damage from another source, they stack.
Fleetfooted
At 4th level, the master infiltrator gains the ability to make quick and silent steps across 
any surface. As a full-round action, he can move up to twice his speed and make Hide and
Move Silently checks without incurring a penalty for moving quickly or across noisy 
surfaces.
At 8th level, this ability progresses further. The master infiltrator ignores difficult terrain 
and becomes able to walk across virtually any surface, even if it would not normally 
support his weight. This enables him to move across liquids like water or lava, and even 
over a forest canopy. When moving this way, the master infiltrator avoids any damage 
from surfaces and hazards that react to being touched, such as lava or caltrops, does not 
trigger mechanical traps with a location trigger, and can walk on slippery and rough 
surfaces without falling. The master infiltrator must end his turn on a surface that can 
support his weight or be exposed to the consequences normally.
Improved Evasion
At 6th level, when wearing light armor or no armor, the master infiltrator takes only half 
damage on a failed save from techniques and abilities that allow a Reflex save for half 
damage, and no damage on a successful save.
Improved Uncanny Dodge
At 7th level, the master infiltrator can no longer be flanked. This defense denies 
opponents the ability to sneak attack the master infiltrator unless the attacker is at least 
four levels higher than the master infiltrator.
Erase Presence
At 10th level, the master infiltrator can erase his presence completely. To do so, he must 
do nothing but concentrate on using the ability for 1 minute. As a result, he erases his 
scent and chakra signature, effectively becoming undetectable to senses that rely on smell
or chakra signatures. This ability lasts until the master infiltrator uses any chakra outside 
of a master infiltrator class ability.
In addition, the master infiltrator can spend 5 chakra while using this ability to make a 
Hide check even when observed, provided he has sufficient cover. This use of chakra is 
considered to be concealed.
Master Infiltrator
At 10th level, the master infiltrator gains the ability to perform well under pressure. He 
can always take 10 when making a Balance, Climb, Disable Device, Hide, Jump, Move 
Silently, Tumble or Swim check, even when stress or distractions would prevent it.
