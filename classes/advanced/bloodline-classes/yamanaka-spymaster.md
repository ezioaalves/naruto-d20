# Yamanaka Spymaster

This clan has developed very useful spying techniques, such as the Shintenshin no Jutsu, and has become very skilled with those techniques. Their mastery at Spy craft is not matched by any other known clan, making them a trump card to Hidden Leaf village.

## Requirements

To qualify to become a yamanaka spymaster, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Skills**:  Bluff 6 ranks, Diplomacy 3 ranks, Genjutsu 3 ranks, Knowledge (Ninja Lore) 3 ranks Ninjutsu 6 ranks.
*   **Techniques**:  At least one Yamanaka Hijutsu.
*   **Special**:  Must be a member of the Yamanaka Clan.

## Class Information

The following information pertains to the Yamanaka Spymaster prestige class.

*   **Hit Die**: The Yamanaka Spymaster gains 1d8 hit points per level. The Constitution modifier applies.
*   **Action Points**: The Yamanaka Spymaster gains a number of action points equal to 6 + one-half his character level, rounded down, every time he attains a new level in this class.
*   **Class Skills**: The Yamanaka Spymaster's class skills are as follows. Bluff (Cha), Craft (Chemical, Calligraphy) (Int), Diplomacy (Cha), Disguise (Cha), Gather Information (Cha), Genjutsu (Cha), Knowledge (Ninja Lore) (Int), Listen (Wis), Ninjutsu (Int), Perform (all), Profession (Wis), Read/Write Language (None), Speak Language (None), Spot (Wis).
*   **Skill Points at Each Level**: 5 + Int modifier.

## Class Features

The following features pertain to the Yamanaka Spymaster prestige class.

### Spymaster

The character can choose one ability from that list every time he gains this ability. The ability can't be chosen twice unless specified. This ability is gained on 1st and 3rd level of Yamanaka Spymaster. Hijutsu Mastery: The Yamanaka Spymaster gains a bonus of +1 to learn and +2 to Perform and Convert Yamanaka Hijutsu to hit points. Many Masks: While under the effect of Shintenshin no Jutsu, the character receives a circumstance bonus of +4 to Bluff and Diplomacy checks. Mind Battle Expertise: The Yamanaka gains a bonus of +1 to the saving throw DC of any Yamanaka Hijutsu that requires a will save, or +2 to a single Yamanaka Hijutsu that requires a will save. This ability can be selected twice, but the effects do not stack. Instead, if the character previously selected the +1 DC bonus to all Yamanaka Hijutsu, he now receives a +2 DC bonus to a single Hijutsu and vice-versa.

### Bonus Feat

Choose a feat from among the following, for which you must meet any prerequisites: Genin, Genjutsu Adept, Chuunin, Jounin, Ninjutsu Adept, Deceptive, Trustworthy, Technique Focus (Yamanaka Hijutsu only).
