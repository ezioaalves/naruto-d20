# Waterbender

The Waterbender is a warrior who has honed her ability to manipulate water or blood.

## Requirements

To qualify to become a waterbender, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Skills**:  Ninjutsu 6 ranks; Swim 3 ranks.
*   **Feats**:  Advanced Bloodline (Ketsuryugan)
*   **Special**:  Blood Rebellion (1d6).

## Class Information

The following information pertains to the Waterbender prestige class.

*   **Hit Die**: The Waterbender gains 1d6 hit points per level. The Constitution modifier applies.
*   **Action Points**: The Waterbender gains a number of action points equal to 6 + one-half his character level, rounded down, every time he attains a new level in this class.
*   **Class Skills**: The Waterbender's class skills are as follows. Chakra Control (Wis), Gamble (Wis), Investigate (Int), Knowledge (Ninja Lore, Physical Sciences) (Int), Listen (Wis), Ninjutsu (Int), Profession (Wis), Read/Write Language (None), Sense Motive (Wis), Speak Language (None), Spot (Wis), Survival (Wis), Swim (Str), Taijutsu (Str), Treat Injury (Wis).
*   **Skill Points at Each Level**: 5 + Int modifier.

## Class Features

The following features pertain to the Waterbender prestige class.

### Aspect Of Streams

The first step to becoming a Waterbender is to understand that all things come from humble beginnings. Even the mighty ocean starts as the tiny stream. The Waterbender enhances her affinity for water, she increases the save DCs of her suiton techniques by +1. Her Blood Rebellion ability also increases by 1d6.

### Aspect Of Rivers

The Waterbender moves closer to total dominion over water, and further enhances her chosen aspect. She gains a bonus to her effective skill threshold to use suiton techniques equal to her Waterbender level. Further, the Waterbender gains Water Resistance equal to her Constitution Score plus her Waterbender level. The Waterbender's Blood Rebellion ability is further modified. If the ability hits, then the Waterbender may choose to do less dice of damage in order to impose a penalty to the attack rolls, damage rolls, and defense of the target. Each die of damage traded away in this manner imposes a -2 penalty to the target instead of doing damage. The penalty lasts a number of rounds equal to the user's Waterbender level, and does not stack with itself.

### Aspect Of Oceans

The Waterbender comes into her own and passes from the rivers into the vast ocean. Her skill with her chosen aspect increases greatly. The Waterbender's skill with suiton techniques further enhances, now she gains bonus damage on each suiton technique equal to her Intelligence modifier. If blood rebellion was the Waterbender's chosen aspect, then she gains the ability to completely manipulate the targets' body. The Waterbender may choose to not do damage when making a blood rebellion attack. Instead, the target must make a Fortitude Save (DC 10 + 1/2 the character’s level + the character’s Wisdom modifier) or act as if affected by the Shinranshin no Jutsu (Betrayal Technique) for a number of rounds equal to the users' Waterbender level. The target still has control of his head and mount as per the Betrayal Technique, but otherwise is unable to control his body. The Waterbender does not need to concentrate in order to use this ability, but must use her move action each round in order to maintain control over the afflicted target.
