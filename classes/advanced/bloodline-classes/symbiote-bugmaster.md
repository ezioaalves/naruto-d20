# Symbiote Bugmaster

Symbiotes are some of the most feared shinobi of all ninja villages, due to their weird abilities to control swarms of chakra draining bugs, the bane of any shinobi.

## Requirements

To qualify to become a symbiote bugmaster, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Skills**:  Chakra Control 4 ranks, Knowledge (Earth and Life Sciences) 4 ranks, Ninjutsu 6 ranks.
*   **Feats**:  Advanced Bloodline (Symbiote).

## Class Information

The following information pertains to the Symbiote Bugmaster prestige class.

*   **Hit Die**: The Symbiote Bugmaster gains 1d6 hit points per level. The Constitution modifier applies.
*   **Action Points**: The Symbiote Bugmaster gains a number of action points equal to 6 + one-half his character level, rounded down, every time he attains a new level in this class.
*   **Class Skills**: The Symbiote Bugmaster's class skills are as follows. Balance (Dex), Chakra Control (Wis), Concentration (Con), Genjutsu (Cha), Gather Information (Cha), Handle Animal (Cha), Hide (Dex), Intimidate (Cha), Investigate (Wis), Jump (Str), Knowledge (Earth and Life Sciences, Ninja Lore, Tactics) (Int), Listen (Wis), Move Silently (Dex), Ninjutsu (Int), Read Language (None), Sense Motive (Wis), Sleight of Hand (Dex), Speak Language (None), Spot (Wis), Survival (Wis), Taijutsu (Str), Tumble (Dex).
*   **Skill Points at Each Level**: 5 + Int modifier.

## Class Features

The following features pertain to the Symbiote Bugmaster prestige class.

### Advanced Symbiote Control

Choose one of the following Masteries. A Mastery cannot be chosen twice unless specified: Bug Toughness: Every swarm under control of this character gains +4 to its Constitution score, this applies to any Hive Mother (requires any other ability of Advanced Symbiote Control list). Extra Swarm: The Symbiote Bugmaster gains one extra swarm of bugs inside his body (requires any other ability of Advanced Symbiote Control list). Improved Bug Reserve: The Bug Reserve ability of the Symbiote bloodline is improved. The character is now able to leave behind up to 1 swarm per 2 levels, 2 weeks per level. Improved Bug CR: The resistance to elements possessed by swarms is increased by 5, the value can exceed the maximum possible Energy Resistance ability of Symbiote swarms. Improved Chakra Drain: The amount of chakra drained by the swarms is increased by 2. Increased Speed: The land, climb, swim, burrow and flying speed of all swarms is increased by 10 ft. Motherly Senses: Hive Mothers improve their Sense Chakra ability to 40 ft. while within their host. Savage Feast: All swarms deal additional damage, see the chart below.

### Intense Breeding

The Symbiote Bugmaster’s ability to breed insects has become legendary, swarms that reside within his body are unique and can only exist within him. Any swarm the Symbiote Bugmaster creates possess two extraordinary abilities of any of the swarms he normally has. For example, the character could give a kikaichuu swarm Scent Marker (Ex) on top of the kikaichuu’s Chakra Leech (Ex), as long as he also has the tracking swarm type. Once two species have been bred together they cannot be separated, and will always share those traits. The species that breeds into another is lost, in the above example the Symbiote’s tracking swarm would be no more and he would have room for one more new type of swarm. If the Symbiote has a minor bloodline, the user may select one swarm type to merge with his only current swarm type. This ability can only be applied once between two types of swarms.

### Bonus Feat

Choose a feat from among the following, for which you must meet any prerequisites: Ability Focus (Symbiote Chakra Drain), Armor Proficiency, Black Death, Bug Host, Chakra Restoration, Chuunin, Destruction Bugs, Genin, Greater Hivemind, Hivemind, Jounin, Rapid Reproduction, Skilled Swarm.
