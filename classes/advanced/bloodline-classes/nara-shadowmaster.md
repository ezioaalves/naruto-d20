# Nara Shadowmaster

## Requirements

To qualify to become a nara shadowmaster, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Skills**:  Hide 3 Ranks, Move Silently 3 Ranks, Ninjutsu 6 Ranks, Knowledge (Ninja Lore or Tactics) 6 Ranks.
*   **Feats**:  Shadow Arts.
*   **Techniques**:  At least one Nara Hijutsu.
*   **Special**:  Must be a member of the Nara Clan.

## Class Information

The following information pertains to the Nara Shadowmaster prestige class.

*   **Hit Die**: The Nara Shadowmaster gains 1d8 hit points per level. The Constitution modifier applies.
*   **Action Points**: The Nara Shadowmaster gains a number of action points equal to 6 + one-half his character level, rounded down, every time he attains a new level in this class.
*   **Class Skills**: The Nara Shadowmaster's class skills are as follows. Balance (Dex), Bluff (Cha), Chakra Control, Craft (Calligraphy), Knowledge (Tactics, Ninja Lore) (Int), Hide (Dex), Listen (Wis), Sleight of Hand, Move Silently (Dex), Ninjutsu (Int), Sense Motive (Wis), Spot (Wis).
*   **Skill Points at Each Level**: 5 + Int modifier.

## Class Features

The following features pertain to the Nara Shadowmaster prestige class.

### Shadow Mastery

Choose one of the following Talents. A Talent cannot be chosen twice unless specified. Kage Mane Expertise: The character is able to hold the creatures for one extra round, regardless of his chakra pool. This ability can be taken twice to grant two extra rounds. Sneak Attack: The character gains the Sneak Attack ability (+1d6). Shadow Arts Expertise (Requires one ability from the list): The character gains a bonus of +1 to Learn, +2 to Perform, convert to Hit Points and Concentration Checks when using a Shadow Art technique.

### Bonus Feat

Choose a feat from among the following, for which you must meet any prerequisites: Armor Proficiency, Deceptive, Focused, Genin, Improved Chakra Pool, Chuunin, Jounin, Ninjutsu Adept, Technique Focus (Nara Hijutsu only).
