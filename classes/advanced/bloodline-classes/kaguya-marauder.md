# Kaguya Marauder

Before being wiped out by the Mist, the Kaguya clan had some of the most bloodthirsty and battle lover warriors of the five great countries. The sight of a group of Kaguya Marauders in the horizon always brings the promise of battle and carnage… and fear.

## Requirements

To qualify to become a kaguya marauder, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Base Attack Bonus**: +3.
*   **Skills**: Taijutsu 6 ranks, Intimidate 3 ranks.
*   **Feats**: Advanced Bloodline (Shikotsumyaku).
*   **Special**: Must be a member of the Kaguya Clan.

## Class Information

The following information pertains to the Kaguya Marauder prestige class.

*   **Hit Die**: The Kaguya Marauder gains 1d10 hit points per level. The Constitution modifier applies.
*   **Action Points**: The Kaguya Marauder gains a number of action points equal to 6 + one-half his character level, rounded down, every time he attains a new level in this class.
*   **Class Skills**: The Kaguya Marauder's class skills are as follows. Balance (Dex), Chakra Control (Wis), Climb, Intimidate (Cha), Jump (Str), Knowledge (Ninja Lore, Tactics) (Int), Listen (Wis), Ninjutsu (Int), Profession (Wis), Spot (Wis), Survival (Wis), Swim (Str), Taijutsu (Str), Tumble (Dex).
*   **Skill Points at Each Level**: 3 + Int modifier.

## Class Features

The following features pertain to the Kaguya Marauder prestige class.

### Bone Armory

The character may now use their Bone Weapon and Bone Armor abilities to create a wider variety of weapons and protection.

*   **Weapons**: All Bone Weapons deal additional damage equal to their Kaguya Marauder level, as well as have a critical threat range of 18-20/x2. The user gains Weapon Focus which applies to all their Bone Weapons.
*   **Armor**: As a full round action the user may also create bone armor that functions as normal armor of its type. When the user can create Small Bone weapons they may make bone armor that grants them +2 Natural Armor, at Medium Bone weapons the user may create bone armor that grants +4 Natural Armor, and at Large Bone weapons the user can create bone armor that grants +6 Natural Armor. This ability costs 4 chakra for each category and lasts for the same amount of time as a Bone Weapon.
