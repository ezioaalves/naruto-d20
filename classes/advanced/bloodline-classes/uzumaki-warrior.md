# Uzumaki Warrior

The Uzumaki Warrior is a gifted shinobi who utilizes his large chakra pool and mastery of the sealing arts to devastate opponents.

## Requirements

To qualify to become a uzumaki warrior, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Base Attack Bonus**:  +3
*   **Skills**:  Chakra Control 8 ranks, Craft (Calligraphy) 6 ranks, Fuinjutsu 8 ranks.
*   **Feats**:  Sealweaver, Fuinjutsu Adept.
*   **Special**:  Must be a member of the Uzumaki Clan.

## Class Information

The following information pertains to the Uzumaki Warrior prestige class.

*   **Hit Die**: The Uzumaki Warrior gains 1d6 hit points per level. The Constitution modifier applies.
*   **Action Points**: The Uzumaki Warrior gains a number of action points equal to 7 + one-half his character level, rounded down, every time he attains a new level in this class.
*   **Class Skills**: The Uzumaki Warrior's class skills are as follows. Balance (Dex), Chakra Control (Wis), Concentration (Con), Craft (Calligraphy), Fuinjutsu (Int), Knowledge (Ninja Lore) (Int), Ninjutsu (Int), Sense Motive (Wis), Spot (Wis), Survival (Wis), Treat Injury (Wis).
*   **Skill Points at Each Level**: 5 + Int modifier.

## Class Features

The following features pertain to the Uzumaki Warrior prestige class.

### Fuinjutsu Mastery

The Uzumaki Warrior gains a +1 enhancement bonus to your effective skill threshold and Learn checks for Fuinjutsu techniques.

### Uzumaki Secret

The Uzumaki clan is renowned for being absolute masters of Fuinjutsu, even called the forefathers of the practice, and this holds true for the Uzumaki Warrior as well. The Uzumaki Warrior can craft Fuinjutsu seals and Advanced seals in half the time indicated (minimum 1 round).

### Power Of The Uzumaki

Once time per day, the Uzumaki Warrior can perform a Fuinjutsu or Chakra Control technique paying half its chakra cost.

### Talents Of The Clan

Whenever a class offers you a Bonus Feat, add Advanced Seal Proficiency, Chakra Restoration, Chakra Presence, Chakra Weaving, Empowered Resilience, Greater Fortitude, and Improved Chakra Pool to the list of available feats.
