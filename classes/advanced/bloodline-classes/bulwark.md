# Bulwark

The Bulwark hones his bloodline to perfection, showing almost no weak points to his enemy at all.

## Requirements

To qualify to become a bulwark, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Base Attack Bonus**:  +3
*   **Skills**:  Taijutsu 8 ranks, Ninjutsu 8 ranks
*   **Feats**:  Advanced Bloodline (Fortify), Harm's Way, Great Fortitude or Empowered Resilience

## Class Information

The following information pertains to the Bulwark prestige class.

*   **Hit Die**: The Bulwark gains 1d12 hit points per level. The Constitution modifier applies.
*   **Action Points**: The Bulwark gains a number of action points equal to 6 + one-half his character level, rounded down, every time he attains a new level in this class.
*   **Class Skills**: The Bulwark's class skills are as follows. Climb (Str), Concentration (Con), Craft (Mechanical, Structural) (Int), Drive (Dex), Intimidate (Cha), Knowledge (Current Events, Popular Culture, Streetwise) (Int), Profession (Wis), Read/Write Language (None), Ride (Dex), Speak Language (None), Spot (Wis), Survival (Wis).
*   **Skill Points at Each Level**: 3 + Int modifier.

## Class Features

The following features pertain to the Bulwark prestige class.

### Indomitable

The Bulwark can spend an action point to reroll a saving throw against any ongoing jutsu, effect or condition that he has failed to save before, adding the result of the action point as a bonus to the roll. If someone from the user's first two allegiance groups are in serious danger (at GM's discretion), the action point bonus doubles.

### Best Defense

The Bulwark gains his Constitution to his damage, with a cap of +2 per Bulwark level.

### Bonus Feat

Choose a feat from among the following, for which you must meet any prerequisites: Advanced Combat Martial Arts, Alertness, Athletic, Brawl, Confident, Defensive Martial Arts, Dodge, Empowered Resilience, Endurance, Genin, Great Fortitude, Improved Brawl, Improved Bull Rush, Improved Chakra Pool, Improved Combat Martial Arts, Improved Feint, Knockout Punch, Nin Weapons Proficiency, Power Attack, Retrieval Expert, Streetfighting, Taijutsu Adept, Talented Shinobi,

### Toughness, Training, Vehicle Expert

The Bulwark gains 25% Fortification, and a +4 saving throw bonus against death effects and Massive Damage saves.
