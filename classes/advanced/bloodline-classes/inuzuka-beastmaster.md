# Inuzuka Beastmaster

The ninja of the Inuzuka clan of Hidden Leaf are shinobi that rely on their friendship to canine companions. This clan created many fearsome techniques, such as Tsuuga and Garouga. Inuzuka Beastmasters know how to teach their companions amazing tricks and combat tactics that other animal clans can only dream of.

## Requirements

To qualify to become a inuzuka beastmaster, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
*   **Base Attack Bonus**:  +2
*   **Skills**:  Handle Animal 6 rank, Ninjutsu 3 ranks.
*   **Feats**:  Moujuu Aishou, Animal Bond.
*   **Techniques**:  At least one Inuzuka Hijutsu.
*   **Special**:  Must be a member of Inuzuka clan.

## Class Information

The following information pertains to the Inuzuka Beastmaster prestige class.

*   **Hit Die**: The Inuzuka Beastmaster gains 1d8 hit points per level. The Constitution modifier applies.
*   **Action Points**: The Inuzuka Beastmaster gains a number of action points equal to 6 + one-half his character level, rounded down, every time he attains a new level in this class.
*   **Class Skills**: The Inuzuka Beastmaster's class skills are as follows. Balance (Dex), Craft (Mechanical), Handle Animal (Cha), Hide (Dex), Intimidate (Cha), Jump (Str), Listen (Wis), Move Silently (Dex), Ninjutsu (Int), Spot (Wis), Survival (Wis), Taijutsu (Str), Tumble (Dex).
*   **Skill Points at Each Level**: 3 + Int modifier.

## Class Features

The following features pertain to the Inuzuka Beastmaster prestige class.

### Animal Stunt

Choose one of the following Talents. A Talent cannot be chosen twice unless specified. Improved Flank: The bonus to attack rolls when the character flanks an enemy with his animal companion is increased by +1 (Total of +3). Animal Sneak Attack: The animal companion gains the Sneak Attack ability (+1d6). This ability can be chosen twice. It's effects stack. Inuzuka Technique Focus: The animal companion gains a bonus of +2 in Ninjutsu and Taijutsu checks to perform any Inuzuka Hijutsu, and the DC of said techniques is increased by +1. Animal Swiftness: The animal companion receives a bonus of +2 to its dexterity score. Animal Brutality: The animal companion receives a bonus of +2 to its strength score. Tough Hide: The animal companion receives a bonus of +2 to its natural armor. This ability can be taken twice. Its effects stack. Animal Companion Bonus Feat: The animal companion gets a bonus feat. The bonus fat must be selected from the following list, and the animal companion must meet all the prerequisites of the feat to select it. Improved Natural Armor, Improved Natural Attack, Dodge, Toughness, Power Attack, Improved Bull Rush. Bonus Feat Choose a feat from among the following, for which you must meet any prerequisites: Animal Bond, Armor Proficiency, Blood Pact (Dog only), Genin, Improved Chakra Pool, Chuunin, Jounin, Technique Focus (Inuzuka Hijutsu only).
