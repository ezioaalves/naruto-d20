# Berserker

The berserker is a surprising foe, not because of his unique tactics or finesse, but because 
of his ability to be completely rational one moment, then fly into a screaming, incoherent 
rage and carve a swath of death and devastation across the battlefield the next. He masters
and embraces his rage, and turns it lethal weapon that merits respect—and fear.

## Requirements:
To qualify to become a berserker, a character must fulfill all the following criteria.
* Base Attack Bonus: +5.
* Feats: [Universal: Risky Strike], Cleave.

## Class Information

The following information pertains to the Berserker prestige class.

* **Hit Die**: The Berserker gains 1d12 hit points per level. The Constitution modifier applies.
* **Action Points**: The Berserker gains a number of action points equal to 7 + one-half his character level, rounded down, everytime he attains a new level in this class.
* **Class Skills**: The Berserker's class skills are as follows. Acrobatics (Dex), Climb (Str), Craft (Int), Intimidate (Cha), Knowledge (Local) (Int), Perception (Wis), Profession (Wis), Ride (Dex), Swim (Str).
 * **Skill Points at Each Level**: 3 + Int modifier.

## Class Features
The following features pertain to the Berserker prestige class.

### Rage
The berserker can let rage fill his mind and enter an almost trance-like state of clarity,  where every moment is a hammer, and every problem a panel of glass. In a rage, the berserker gains a +4 bonus to Strength and Constitution, a +2 morale bonus on Will saves and Intimidate checks, but takes a –2 penalty to Defense. While raging, the berserker  cannot use Dexterity-, Intelligence- or Charisma-based skills (except for Balance, Drive,  Escape Artist, Intimidate and Ride), the Concentration skill, or perform any task that  requires attention or concentration, or cast spells, manifest powers or perform Chakra  Control, Fuinjutsu, Genjutsu or Ninjutsu techniques. The berserker retains use of all feats except [Universal: Defensive Stance], item creation feats and meta-chakra feats. The effects of rage stack with those from any frenzy ability the berserker may have.
The berserker can prematurely end his rage. At the end of the rage, he becomes fatigued for the rest of the encounter. Otherwise, the rage lasts for a number of rounds equal to 3 + his improved Constitution modifier. The berserker can use this ability once per day at 1st level, then an additional time per day at 3rd and 5th level.

### Uncanny Dodge
The berserker is never caught unprepared for battle, and keeps his wits about him even in  the most unfavorable situations. He retains his Dexterity bonus to Defense when caught flat-footed or struck by an unseen opponent.

### Greater Rage
At 5th level, the berserker gains such clarity from his rage that it is more akin to a  wrecking ball than a hammer. The bonus provided by his Rage class ability increase to +6 to Strength and Constitution, +4 morale bonus to Will saves, and the penalty to Defense  is unchanged.

### Bonus Feat
At 2nd and 4th level, the Berserker gets a bonus feat. The bonus feat must be selected from the following list, and the Berserker must meet all the prerequisites of the feat to select it.
* Archaic Weapons Proficiency
* Combat Reflexes
* Great Cleave, 
* Powerful Maneuvers
* Improved Risky Strike
* Weapon Focus.
